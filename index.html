Pending vs available rewards

Available rewards are cleared balance. Pending rewards are waiting for provider callback, hold period, duplicate checks, or manual fraud review.

Payout methods

Why rewards may pend or reverse

Rewards may pend or reverse because of advertiser review, provider chargeback, duplicate account activity, VPN/proxy abuse, failed offer requirements, or fraud review.
